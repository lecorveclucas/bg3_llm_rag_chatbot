Find Doni : Find Doni is a Quest in Baldur's Gate 3. Find Doni can be acquired during Act 1 and takes place in Druid Grove. Completing Quests allows players to learn more about the world and characters in Baldur's Gate 3, as well as earning more loot and experience to become more powerful.   

Find Doni Objectives : As the tieflings prepare for the siege, Rikka asked us to find Doni and bring him to safety.
