Get Gortash's Netherstone : Get Gortash's Netherstone is a Quest in Baldur's Gate 3. Get Gortash's Netherstone can be acquired during Act 3 as part of Confront the Elder Brain. Complete this Quest to obtain one of the Three Netherstones that you need to collect.    

Get Gortash's Netherstone Objectives : Gortash left for Baldur's Gate. We should go after him. Baldur's Gate is near. We should find out where Gortash is We discovered that Gortash is in Wyrm's Rock Fortress, where he will be sworn in as an Archduke of Baldur's Gate. We need to make our way there.  We made it to Wyrm's Rock Fortress and were invited to Gortash's inauguration upstairs. Gortash is protected by the Steel Watch. if we want to make a move against him, we should figure out how to deal with them first.  Gortash put forward a proposition. if we kill Orin and take her Netherstone, he will let us rule the city alongside him with the elder brain at our command.   

How to unlock Get Gortash's Netherstone : Automatically gained at the start of Act 3.  

Get Gortash's Netherstone Rewards : Rewards  

Get Gortash's Netherstone Walkthrough : If you visit the Sword Coast Couriers in Rivington, you'll find a poster you can look at freely on the desk - the Gortash Coronation Poster. If you read it, you'll learn that Gortash is being named an Archduke and that the ceremony will take place at Wyrm's Rock. Get Gortash's Netherstone and Our Fiery Friend will be updated with this information. After you disabled the steel watches, you can venture to Wyrm's Rock upper level to fight Gortash. After you defeated him, loot his body for the gauntlets to get the Netherstone.

Get Gortash's Netherstone Notes & Tips : Gortash has a +0 to Constitution saving throws. Despite him having advantage on spell saving throws, Flesh to Stone is an excellent spell to use on him, especially when paired with a Hex (CON).
