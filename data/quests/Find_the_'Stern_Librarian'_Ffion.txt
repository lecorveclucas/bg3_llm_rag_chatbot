Find the 'Stern Librarian' Ffion : Find the 'Stern Librarian' Ffion is a Quest in Baldur's Gate 3. Find the 'Stern Librarian' Ffion can be acquired during Act 3.. Completing Quests allows players to learn more about the world and characters in Baldur's Gate 3, as well as earning more loot and experience to become more powerful.   

Find the 'Stern Librarian' Ffion Objectives : We found Ffion's body. We should tell Mamzell Amira
We found the corpse of a dwarf called Ffion. We were told that she used to work for the Mamzell in Sharess' Caress. Perhaps we should tell the Mamzell. 
Mamzell Amira was heartbroken to learn of Ffion's fate. For our troubles, she has gifted us some private time with the drow twins that work for her. We can find them in the taproom. 
 

Find the 'Stern Librarian' Ffion Walkthrough : Upon reaching the top floor of the Flaygo's Flophouse in X:-16 Y:76, you'll observe blood stains near a bed. You have the option to interact with this scene by selecting:
[Investigation] Attempt to identify the origin of the blood..Leave.
Opt for the first choice, and with sufficient intelligence, you'll deduce the presence of a concealed body beneath the bed, adjacent to the blood stains. Extract the body to reveal Ffion's remains, obtaining Ffion's key in the process. Following the search, engage with Ffion's corpse using the Speak with the Dead spell. Pose the following questions:
Who are you?.What is this location?.How did your demise occur?.What were your activities before your passing?.Leave.
Select the third option to uncover that his son was responsible for his murder. Proceed by inquiring about his son's identity, to which he responds with the name "Dolor." Continue by questioning the reason for his son's actions, and he reveals that he had discovered his son's hidden transgressions. Finally, query the nature of his son's secret, learning that his son had a penchant for taking lives.
Approach Mamzell Amira at Sharess' Caress at X:10 Y:82. She will deduce your purpose as related to Ffion and express sorrow for her absence. If you delve further into the matter, the quest "Find the 'Stern Librarian' Ffion" will be triggered, but you've already discovered the body. Inform Mamzell immediately, and she will mourn Ffion's fate while expressing greater concern for financial matters. You can question her with options like:
A woman has lost her life, and your focus is on money?.Perhaps a small reward could be spared for me..Let's discuss other matters.
Upon selecting the first option, she rationalizes that death is commonplace in Baldur's Gate and emphasizes her business priorities. Subsequently, inquire about the possibility of receiving a reward, and she will direct your attention to the drow twins in the taproom. She provides you with a key to the Sharess' Caress Rooms and suggests you introduce yourself as a gift from Mamzell to witness their legendary status among patrons. This key acquisition concludes the "Find the 'Stern Librarian' Ffion" quest as well.
 

 How to unlock Find the 'Stern Librarian' Ffion : Mamzell Amira, Sharess' Caress
 

Find the 'Stern Librarian' Ffion Rewards : Quality time with the drow twins, Sorn Orlith and Nym Orlith.
