Our Fiery Friend : Our fiery friend is a Companion Quest in Baldur's Gate 3. Our fiery friend is auto-acquired when you recruit Karlach.  Completing Our fiery friend will progress Karlach story progression forward.   

Our fiery friend Objectives : Deal with the Paladins of Tyr.Travel with Karlach.Find safe harbour and speak to Karlach.Find Lord Gortash.Confront Lord Gortash.Kill Lord Gortash.Take Karlach to Gortash's corpse.Continue travelling with Karlach.
