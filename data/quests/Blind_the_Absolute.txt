Blind the Absolute : Blind the Absolute is a Quest in Baldur's Gate 3. Blind the Absolute can be acquired during Act 1. This quest takes place in the Underdark as part of the Free True Soul Nere Main Quest.    

Blind the Absolute Objectives : We agreed to help Brithvar and his group double-cross Nere. First, we need to take out the scrying eye - discreetly.
