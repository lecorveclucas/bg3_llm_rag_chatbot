Retrieve Omeluum : Retrieve Omeluum is a Quest in Baldur's Gate 3. Retrieve Omeluum can be acquired during Act 3.    

Retrieve Omeluum Objectives : We spoke to a hobgoblin called Blurg in the society of Brilliance Lodge. His illithid companion Omeluum is missing - we agreed to look for it.   

Retrieve Omeluum Walkthrough : If you sold the Githyanki egg in quest Steal a Githyanki Egg, the newborn githyanki will slaughter members of the lodge, blocking this quest.Omeluum is kept in Iron Throne as a prisoner. You can only reach the place from Flymm Cargo. (Quest Save the Gondians and Avenge the Drowned will lead you to this location.) (Hatch to Flymm Cargo is hidden beneath some crates, at X-163, Y-143. Alternatively, you can reach Flymm Cargo basement through City Sewers.).You need to carefully select your team and spells for this location. Once you land on Iron Throne, you will have only 6 turns to extract as many prisoners as possible. Dimensional door, and Teleportation Circle spells are very helpful, as well as Potion of Speed.Send one of your tank teammates to rescue him, and draw the attention of Sahuagin. Omeluum has a special spell that can teleport himself and one extra back to the sub, so don't worry about the time.  

 How to unlock Retrieve Omeluum : Blurg in Lodge
