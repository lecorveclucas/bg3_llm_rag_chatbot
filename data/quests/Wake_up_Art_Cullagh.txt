Wake up Art Cullagh : Wake up Art Cullagh is a Quest in Baldur's Gate 3. Wake up Art Cullagh can be acquired during Act 2. Completing Quests allows players to learn more about the world and characters in Baldur's Gate 3, as well as earning more loot and experience to become more powerful.   

Wake up Art Cullagh Objectives : Find some clues about Art..Tell Halsin about Art Cullagh..Investigate the House of Healing..Show the lute to Art.
 

Wake up Art Cullagh Walkthrough : Enter the Last Light Inn, then enter the door on the right to find Art Cullagh in a catatonic state. Cullagh repeats a song about "Thaniel". (Halsin will tell you more about Thaniel if he's alive.).Speak to Art and to the NPCs (Jeh'lar) around him to receive the next objective (read the worn letter to learn that Art Cullagh was gripped by shadowfell after his investigation of House of Healing), to investigate the House of Healing. .Return to camp to alert Halsin about the situation..Go to the House of Healing and deal with Malus Thorm and his nurses. You can pass a check (Persuasion DC16, Religion DC 14, Investigation DC 14) to suggest the sisters practise on each other. You can also persuade Malus Thorm to commit suicide (Persuasion DC21), if you read the book Surgery and Physiology: A Sharran's Primer in a room to the north, the DC is slightly lower..After dealing with Malus and the sisters, take the lute from Malus after completing this step..Return to the Last Light Inn and play the lute for Art..Meet Halsin by the lakeside if he is with you. (Quest Lift the Shadow Curse)
 

 How to unlock Wake up Art Cullagh : Subquest of Lift the Shadow Curse.
 

Wake up Art Cullagh Rewards : Rewards
 

Wake up Art Cullagh Notes & Tips : Note: proceeding too far in the Act 2 story quest Infiltrate Moonrise Towers will cause Art to die, leaving this quest unable to be completed.
