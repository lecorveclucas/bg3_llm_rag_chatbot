Destroy the Ancient Tome : Destroy the Ancient Tome is a Quest in Baldur's Gate 3. Destroy the Ancient Tome can be acquired during Act 1 and is a part of the Search the Cellar. Completing Quests allows players to learn more about the world and characters in Baldur's Gate 3, as well as earning more loot and experience to become more powerful.
