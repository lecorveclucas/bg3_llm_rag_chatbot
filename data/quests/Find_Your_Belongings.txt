Find Your Belongings : Find your Belongings is a Quest in Baldur's Gate 3. Find your Belongings can be acquired during Chapter 1.    

Find your Belongings Objectives : Speak to the thieves..Find the thieves' hideout..Find our stolen belongings..Check the pickpocket's bag.
 

Find your Belongings Walkthrough : You can learn the entrance to Tiefling Hideout by talking to Doni after completing the quest Investigate the Beach.
 

 How to unlock Find your Belongings : Tiefling Hideout
