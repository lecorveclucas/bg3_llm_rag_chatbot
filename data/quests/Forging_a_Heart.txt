Forging a Heart : Forging a Heart is a Quest in Baldur's Gate 3. Forging a Heart can be acquired during Act TBA. This quest is part of the Personal Quests that you can get in the game. It is a subquest of Servant of None. Completing Quests allows players to learn more about the world and characters in Baldur's Gate 3, as well as earn more loot and experience to become more powerful.   

Forging a Heart Objectives : Dammon, an infernal mechanic, told me to find some infernal iron. He might be able to repair my heart.
