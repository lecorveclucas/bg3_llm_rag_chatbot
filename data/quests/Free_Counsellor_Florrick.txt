Free Counsellor Florrick : Free Counsellor Florrick is a Quest in Baldur's Gate 3. Free Counsellor Florrick can be acquired during Act 3. This quest can be obtained when you reach Baldur's Gate. All Baldur's Gate quests will be grouped together in your quest journal.     

Free Counsellor Florrick Objectives : We learned of Counsellor Florrick's impending execution, but not her exact location. If we want her help, we should find where she's being kept.   

Free Counsellor Florrick Walkthrough : Counsellor Florrick is kept as a prisoner in Wyrmrock Prison. You need to sneak into the prison and break her free.You can sneak into the prison through a hole in the western wall of the Wyrm's Rock. .There are flaming fists patrolling the prison. Use one of your characters in Turn-based mode to Florrick's cell, lockpick the door and escort her out.Florrick is a potential ally in Gather Your Allies. (Summon Florrick's personal guard - highly defensive warriors who can hold their own in melee.)  

 How to unlock Free Counsellor Florrick : Duke Ravengard  

Free Counsellor Florrick Rewards : Florrick as a potential ally in Gather Your Allies.
