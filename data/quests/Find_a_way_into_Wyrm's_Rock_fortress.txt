Find a way into Wyrm's Rock fortress : Find a way into Wyrm's Rock fortress is a Quest in Baldur's Gate 3. Find a way into Wyrm's Rock fortress can be acquired during Act 3. This quest can be obtained when you reach Baldur's Gate. All Baldur's Gate quests will be grouped together in your quest journal.    

Find a way into Wyrm's Rock fortress Objectives : Wyrm's Rock is closed due to the ordination of Gortash - who is about to become Archduke of Baldur's Gate. There must be someone, or something, in Wyrm's Crossing that could help us gain entry.   

Find a way into Wyrm's Rock Fortress Walkthrough : Before confrontation with Gortash, Dark Urge can walk in through the gate by speaking to Blaze Elin at the gate. Alternatively, you can get a pass from Valeria (Solve the Open Hand Temple Murders), or from Arfur Gregario, the Toymaker (Investigate the Suspicious Toys). Get A report on Duke Ravengard at Sword Coast Couriers. Or simply convince the Guards to let you in.Once you break into Iron Throne and save the Gondians, the Wyrm's Rock Fortress is closed by Gortash's order.You can jump to the surrounding areas of the fortress, then climb to the main level of the fortress.
