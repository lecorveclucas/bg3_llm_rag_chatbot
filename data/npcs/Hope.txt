Hope : Hope is an NPC in Baldur's Gate 3. Hope can be found at House of Hope. There is a massive amount of NPCs in Baldur's Gate 3, and their ideals, needs and way of living are strictly related to the actions they perform during the course of the game. Their attitude towards you and your party may be affected by the deeds and decisions you and your party have taken on.   

Where to find Hope : Can be found at: Act 3 House of Hope  

Hope Related Quests : Save Hope  

Hope Dialogue Options : Once freed she will become a temporary companion to help you defeat Raphael. Complete Save Hope and she will reward you with the Gloves of Soul Catching.
