Mamzell Amira :   Mamzell Amira is an NPC in Baldur's Gate 3. Mamzell Amira can be found at  Wyrm's Crossing. Mamzell Amira is the owner of Sharess' Caress. There is a massive amount of NPCs in Baldur's Gate 3, and their ideals, needs and way of living are strictly related to the actions they perform during the course of the game. Their attitude towards you and your party may be affected by the deeds and decisions you and your party have taken on.   

Where to find Mamzell Amira : Can be found at: Act 3 Sharess' Caress, Wyrm's Crossing  

Mamzell Amira Related Quests : Find the 'Stern Librarian' Ffion  

Mamzell Amira Dialogue Options : Approach Mamzell Amira at Sharess' Caress at X:10 Y:82. She will deduce your purpose as related to Ffion and express sorrow for her absence. If you delve further into the matter, the quest "Find the 'Stern Librarian' Ffion" will be triggered, but you've already discovered the body. Inform Mamzell immediately, and she will mourn Ffion's fate while expressing greater concern for financial matters. You can question her with options like: A woman has lost her life, and your focus is on money?.Perhaps a small reward could be spared for me.Let's discuss other matters.  Upon selecting the first option, she rationalizes that death is commonplace in Baldur's Gate and emphasizes her business priorities. Subsequently, inquire about the possibility of receiving a reward, and she will direct your attention to the drow twins in the taproom. She provides you with a key to the Sharess' Caress Rooms and suggests you introduce yourself as a gift from Mamzell to witness their legendary status among patrons. This key acquisition concludes the "Find the 'Stern Librarian' Ffion" quest as well.
