Filro the Forgotten : Level 4 Filro the Forgotten is a Boss in Baldur's Gate 3. Filro the Forgotten is one of the three Drow aware of the presence of the Adamantine Forge. Bosses in BG3 are powerful enemies that have increased health and pose a bigger challenge for players.   

Where to find Filro the Forgotten : Act 1 Underdark  

Filro the Forgotten Quests : The Adamantine Forge  

Filro the Forgotten Dialogue Options : Filro the Forgotten becomes hostile on sight and does not allow speaking with him.Once killed, players can use a Scroll of Speak with Dead to learn more about the Forge from him. He will tell you about the other two drows that were looking for the forge, as well as learn about how the Hook Horrors pack adopted him.

Filro the Forgotten Boss Tips : Filro the Forgotten is a mage that prefers to fight at a distance, while the pack of Hook Horrors at his side gets up close and personal with your party. It is a good idea to focus on the Hook Horrors first, and try to take them one by one. Having Glut on your party while taking on this fight might be beneficial. If the Bulette emerges, it may attack the Hook Horrors as well as drawing attention from them. Use this distraction to your advantage!
