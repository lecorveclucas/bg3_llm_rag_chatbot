Salazon : Salazon is an NPC in Baldur's Gate 3. Salazon can be found at Waukeen's Rest. Salazon guards the entrance to the Zhentarim Hideout basement, the trading location for Find the Missing Shipment. There is a massive amount of NPCs in Baldur's Gate 3, and their ideals, needs and way of living are strictly related to the actions they perform during the course of the game. Their attitude towards you and your party may be affected by the deeds and decisions you and your party have taken on.   

Where to find Salazon : Can be found at: Act 1 Waukeen's Rest  

Salazon Related Quests : Find the Missing Shipment  

Salazon Dialogue Options : Salazon will be the man. crouching in between the shelves in Waukeen's Rest.  Recite Rugan's pass-phase: 'Little serpent, long shadow'.[DEXTERITY[ Wait - listen!.[DETECT THOUGHTS] Read his mind.Prepare to attack. If you are there to pass the cargo, tell him the pass-phrase. He'll give you the Engraved Key. It opens the entrance to the Zhentarim Hideout behind the wardrobe. This will advance Find the Missing Shipment. Give the unopened chest to Zarys in the Zhentarim Hideout to receive gold and the rare crossbow "Harold" for your efforts and honesty.
