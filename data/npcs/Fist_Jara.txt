Fist Jara : Fist Jara is an NPC in Baldur's Gate 3. Fist Jara can be found at Sharess' Caress, Wyrm's Crossing. Initially, Fist Jara seems to be part of the Pleasure House, after recognizing you, she transforms into a Mind Flayer. There is a massive amount of NPCs in Baldur's Gate 3, and their ideals, needs and way of living are strictly related to the actions they perform during the course of the game. Their attitude towards you and your party may be affected by the deeds and decisions you and your party have taken on.   

Where to find Fist Jara : Can be found at: Act 3 Sharess' Caress, Wyrm's Crossing. Upper Floor, utilize the key provided by Mamzell Amira to unlock a door. Upon entering, a private scene unfolds between Fist Jara and Naoise Nallinto  

Fist Jara Related Quests : N/A  

Fist Jara Dialogue Options : As you appear, Jara senses something and recognizes you. Reply with: You're in danger. Leave now!.Looks intriguing. Mind if I join?.Whoever you think I am, you're mistaken.This seems personal. I should depart. Select the first option, leading Jara to mention that the Absolute showed her your face. She transforms into a Mind Flayer, triggering a battle.
