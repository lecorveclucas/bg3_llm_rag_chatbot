Okta : Okta is an NPC in Baldur's Gate 3. Okta can be found at The Hollow (Druid Grove). Okta is a cooking Tiefling. There is a massive amount of NPCs in Baldur's Gate 3, and their ideals, needs and way of living are strictly related to the actions they perform during the course of the game. Their attitude towards you and your party may be affected by the deeds and decisions you and your party have taken on. 

Okta Information: : Okta is a cooking Tiefling that is grateful for your valiant effort repelling the invading goblins.

Where to find Okta : Can be found at: Act 1 The Hollow (Druid Grove) .Act 1.The Hollow (Druid Grove)

Okta Dialogue Options : After showing her support to you, Okta offers you a bowl of what she's cooking. Of several option picking "A bowl of grey goo. Reminds me of home" will result in Okta giving you two bowls of Gruel (3d4 healing).
