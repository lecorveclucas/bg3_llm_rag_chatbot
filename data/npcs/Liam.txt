Liam : Liam is an NPC in Baldur's Gate 3. Liam can be found at Shattered Sanctum. Liam is being held captive and tortured by Torturer Spike. There is a massive amount of NPCs in Baldur's Gate 3, and their ideals, needs and way of living are strictly related to the actions they perform during the course of the game. Their attitude towards you and your party may be affected by the deeds and decisions you and your party have taken on.   

Liam Information: : Liam is being held and tortured by goblins to give out the location of the Druid Grove.  

Where to find Liam : Can be found at: Act 1.Shattered Sanctum  

Liam Quests : Save the refugees.Removing the parasite  

Liam Dialogue Options : You must must either fail to Torture Liam or after successfully torturing him, give him a health potion and convince him you are in his side to have the dialogue options below. When you interact with Liam he begs you not to hurt him. You can say: I came for the Nightsong. Where is it?.I'm looking for a druid, name of Halsin.Relax, I'm not going to leave you here.[SLEIGHT OF HAND] Pick the lock. .Leave.  Ask any questions and he will answer.  You have time to ask about Nightsong and Halsin. Saying this to Liam, he states that Nightsong is somewhere in a hidden passage underneath the temple, and that he can't tell you where the Druid Grove is or the goblins will kill everyone.   He asks you to release him and you can tell him that you will not leave him here. Doing so will allow you to respond with the following: [SLEIGHT OF HAND] Pick the lock.Be patient - I'll be back.Leave If you pick the lock and let him go, you can continue to converse with him and ask him questions you need answers to. When you choose to leave, he will run off toward the south.
