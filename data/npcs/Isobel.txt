Isobel : Isobel is an NPC in Baldur's Gate 3. Isobel can be found at the Last Light Inn during Act II. Isobel is a High Half-Elf and Servant of Selune that maintains the Last Light Inn's protection from the Shadow Curse. There is a massive amount of NPCs in Baldur's Gate 3, and their ideals, needs and way of living are strictly related to the actions they perform during the course of the game. Their attitude towards you and your party may be affected by the deeds and decisions you and your party taken on. She is the daughter of Ketheric Thorm and the lover of Aylin.   

Where to find Isobel : Can be found at: Act 2 Last Light Inn  

Isobel Related Quests : Seek Protection from the Shadow Curse  

Isobel Dialogue Options : She can be found on her room’s balcony in the main building. Speaking to her will grant you the condition “Blessing of Selûne”, which protects against the lesser effects of the shadow curse.  Ending the conversation with Isobel will lead to an interaction with Flaming Fist Marcus, who tries to convince Isobel to come with him to see Ketheric.
