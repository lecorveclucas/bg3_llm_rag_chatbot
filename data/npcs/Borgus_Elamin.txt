Borgus Elamin : Borgus Elamin is an NPC in Baldur's Gate 3. Borgus Elamin can be found at Sharess' Caress. There is a massive amount of NPCs in Baldur's Gate 3, and their ideals, needs and way of living are strictly related to the actions they perform during the course of the game. Their attitude towards you and your party may be affected by the deeds and decisions you and your party have taken on.   

Where to find Borgus Elamin : Can be found at: Act 3 Sharess' Caress  

Borgus Elamin Related Quests : Aid the Underduke  

Borgus Elamin Dialogue Options : Upon conversing with Mamzell, you'll spot two men near a door in the brothel engaged in a discussion about Nine-Fingers. Approach them and select the first option, indicating you have business related to her. Borgus will express surprise, remarking that it seems she's involved with everyone. He mentions that the girl has encountered a formidable adversary, and Borgus Elamin queries if it's the Kingpin. Borgus Elamin pledges his blade to support the Kingpin if he intends to confront Nine-Fingers. As Borgus Elamin is about to disclose Borgus' dealings with someone new, he notices your presence and questions you. Respond with: [Persuasion] That sounds like an intriguing opportunity. I'd like to join.[Detect thoughts] Probe the Dwarf's mind.And? Private matters shouldn't be aired in a brothel.Attack.Leave. Opt for the second option to uncover that Nine-Fingers is not pleased with Zenovia and wants to avoid informing her. To address this situation, you can select the option that requires speaking to Nine-Fingers about Zenovia. Borgus Elamin provides the address: Lower City, Basilisk Gate, Guildhall. This updates the "Aid the Underduke" quest.
