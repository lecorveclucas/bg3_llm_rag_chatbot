Haseid : Haseid is an Enemy in Baldur's Gate 3. In BG3, each type of Enemy has different attacks, resistances and immunities.  Players must defeat Enemies to advance Quests, earn experience or obtain loot. He is part of a grave robbing band, consisting of nine other members (Gimblebock, Taman, Quelenna, Warryn, Andorn, Torgga, Barton, Mari, and Cefrey). They are currently in the process of looting the Dank Crypt but aren't having much success.
