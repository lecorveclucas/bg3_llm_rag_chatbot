Remira : Remira is an NPC in Baldur's Gate 3. Remira can be found at the gates of the Druid Grove. Remira is one of the adventurers that travel with Aradin. There is a massive amount of NPCs in Baldur's Gate 3, and their ideals, needs and way of living are strictly related to the actions they perform during the course of the game. Remira Dialogue Options : Remira can be found at the entrance of the Druid Grove, alongside her partners Aradin and Barth.
