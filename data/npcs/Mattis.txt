Mattis : Mattis is an NPC and a Merchant in Baldur's Gate 3. Mattis can be found at the Druid Grove. He is a Tiefling child that proclaims to sell invaluable goods.       

Where to find Mattis : Can be found at: Act 1 Druid Grove Act 2 Last Light Inn ( Saved the Tieflings in Act 1, Mattis did not die in Act 1)

Mattis Related Quests : N/A

If you play along, then he will soon offer to show you his wares.  Mattis is a Tiefling child that sells Rings, that according to him are lucky rings but actually have no real power. If you prod him, he will admit that they're not real lucky rings. In addition to these rings, Mattis also sells assorted miscellaneous junk items.
