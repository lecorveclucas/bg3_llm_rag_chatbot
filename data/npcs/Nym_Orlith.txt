Nym Orlith : Nym Orlith is an NPC in Baldur's Gate 3. Nym Orlith can be found at Sharess' Caress Pleasure House in Wyrm's Crossing. Nym Orlith is a Seldarine Drow, you can find her next to her twin Sorn Orlith, offering their services.
