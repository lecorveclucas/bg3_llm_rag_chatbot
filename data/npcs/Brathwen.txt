Brathwen : Brathwen is an NPC in Baldur's Gate 3. Brathwen can be found at Grymforge. Brathwen is found pushing corpses of dead miners into the lake.   

Brathwen Information: : Brathwen is found with another duergar pushing dead bodies into the lake.Brathwen tells you that those people died due to rock fall, they were miners, and the survivors are still mining, digging True Soul Nere out of the wreckage.You can loot the corpses without going into battle.  

Where to find Brathwen : Can be found at: Act 1.Grymforge
