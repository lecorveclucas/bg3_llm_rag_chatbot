Elf : High Elf, Wood Elf Elf  is a Race in Baldur's Gate 3. Elves feature 4 Proficiencies such as Longsword, Shortsword, Longbow, and Shortbow. While creating your Character in Baldur's Gate 3, you can choose between 11 different types of Races. Each of them has their own special characteristics. With ethereal countenances and long lifespans, elves are at home with nature's power, flourishing in light and dark alike.   

Elf Traits & Features in Baldur's Gate 3 : Base Racial Speed: Move up to 9m per turn.Darkvision: See in the dark up to 12m.Elven Weapon Training: Proficiency with the Longsword, Shortsword, Shortbow, and Longbow.Fey Ancestry: Advantage on Saving Throws against being Charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep.Keen Senses: Proficiency in the Perception skill.
